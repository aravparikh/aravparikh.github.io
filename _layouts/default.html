🌟 Featured Project

ChronicPain AI Assistant

The Problem: Chronic pain affects over 50 million Americans, yet misdiagnosis rates remain alarmingly high, leading to ineffective treatments and patient suffering.

My Solution: I developed an intelligent questionnaire system powered by XGBoost that analyzes patient responses to detect potential misdiagnosis risks with 87% accuracy. The system is deployed on Hugging Face Spaces, making it accessible to healthcare providers and patients worldwide.

Impact: This tool could help doctors catch misdiagnoses earlier, potentially improving treatment outcomes for millions of chronic pain patients and reducing healthcare costs by billions annually.
